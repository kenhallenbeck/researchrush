Vidreya Exits Stealth Mode To Announce Debut Game Beta and Release Window

Research Rush Beta begins today, Full Release in 2024

May 27, 2024 FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

MILLINGTON, New Jersey. - Vidreya, LLC today exited a 3-year incubation period to announce the studio's debut game, Research Rush, will release in 2024.

Research Rush is a casual science lab simulator developed for Android and iOS. The game captures the time crunch, strategy, and research luck core to the biotechnology economy and packages the experience in a casual and fun mobile game.

The Open Beta period begins today and runs until June 31, 2024. The Open Beta is available only on Android.

"Opening Research Rush for public testing marks a significant step forward as we work toward a full release later this year," said Kenneth Hallenbeck, PhD, Vidreya Principal and lead developer of Research Rush. "I am proud of what we've built and am excited to share it with our testers and use their feedback to improve the game".

Anyone can volunteer as a Beta Tester by downloading Research Rush on the Google Play Store.

Google Play Link

Vidreya acquires the rights to Research Rush as part of its exit from incubation within software investment firm TerraPrime, LLC.

As part of the transition, TerraPrime will take an undisclosed stake in Vidreya in exchange for continued capital and operations support for the development of Research Rush.
